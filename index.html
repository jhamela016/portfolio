Who I Am

A little bit about me

Hi! I'm Jhamela — a sixteen-year-old from the quiet barangay of Masi in Sta. Teresita, Cagayan. I find peace in putting words on a page and energy in diving for a volleyball.

Growing up in the Philippines has given me a deep love for the ocean, warm light, and the creatures that share our world — especially cats and dogs. Music is my constant companion, and I believe every sunset is worth stopping for.
